Why do we do this?

Our Vision

In Season 2 of MDA, our mission is to help students identify the medical field that suits them best in a simple and direct way. Building on the success of our first season, we're launching a stronger and more engaging experience. We've delivered scientific content through innovative methods, published a comprehensive book on body systems and common diseases, produced clear and engaging educational videos, and provided hands-on training for each track to help members develop real skills. This season, we aim to guide students through well-structured curricula that explain the differences between medicine, pharmacy, dentistry, and physical therapy, supported by expert speakers and interactive learning experiences that make complex concepts easy to understand.
